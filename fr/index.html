We develop theoretical frameworks for understanding quantum systems at Sherbrooke University. Our research bridges fundamental quantum mechanics with practical applications in chemistry and materials science.

Research Areas

We study quantum statistics of many-body quantum systems, chemical reaction kinetics, and quantum dynamics in open system settings.

Our Team

We are building a research group with diverse academic and personal backgrounds who are passionate about advancing our understanding of quantum systems.
