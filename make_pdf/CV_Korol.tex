\section{\sc Theses}

Undergraduate Thesis: University of Toronto  \hfill{2016} 

\vspace*{-3mm}
Supervisor: Professor D. James Donaldson

\vspace*{-3mm}
Nitrate photolysis in snow: Influence of temperature and salt concentration on gas phase products  

\vspace*{-3mm}
\textit{Nitrate impacts the oxidative capacity of the atmosphere, resulting in Arctic tropospheric ozone. I provide experimental evidence of the influence of the snow surface brine as a key nitrate photolysis source, and show that halide ions previously presumed to be spectators actually scavenge OH radicals. }
